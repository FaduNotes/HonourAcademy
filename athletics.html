TRACK EVENTS - Running

1. Sprints (100m, 200m, 400m)

Start: Crouch start with starting blocks mandatory

False Start Rule: 1 false start = Direct Disqualification (new IAAF rule)

Lane Rule: 100m-400m mein apni lane mein rehna compulsory

Finish: Torso (chest) finish line cross karna - head/hand nahi

Timing: Electronic timing - 0.01 second tak accurate

2. Middle Distance (800m, 1500m)

  • 800m: First bend tak apni lane, phir break kar sakte ho
  • 1500m: Waterfall start - sab ek saath, no lanes
  • Tactics: Pace judgment, positioning important

3. Long Distance (5000m, 10000m)

  • 12.5 laps (5000m) ya 25 laps (10000m)
  • Water stations allowed after 5km
  • Lapped runners ko inner lane deni padti hai

4. Hurdles

110m Hurdles (Men): 10 hurdles, 1.067m height

100m Hurdles (Women): 10 hurdles, 0.838m height

400m Hurdles: 10 hurdles, 0.914m (M) / 0.762m (W)

Rule: Hurdle gira sakte ho, penalty nahi. But deliberately knock down kiya to DQ

5. Relays (4x100m, 4x400m)

  • Baton Exchange: 20m exchange zone mein karna
  • Dropped Baton: Utha sakte ho but time waste hoga
  • Zone Violation: Exchange zone ke bahar kiya to DQ
  • 4x100m: Sab apni lane mein
  • 4x400m: First leg apni lane, phir break

FIELD EVENTS - Jumping

1. Long Jump

Runway: 40m long

Takeoff Board: 20cm wide, white with plasticine

Foul: Board ke aage se jump kiya, ya landing ke baad back walk kiya

Measurement: Board se nearest mark in sand

Attempts: 3 trials, top 8 ko 3 aur attempts

2. High Jump

  • Fosbury Flop: Back-first technique - sab use karte hai ab
  • 3 attempts: Har height pe 3 chance
  • Fail: Bar gira diya ya bar ke neeche se gaye
  • Tie-Breaker: Kam attempts mein clear kiya wo jeetega

3. Triple Jump (Hop, Step, Jump)

  • Takeoff board se Hop → Step → Jump sequence
  • Same leg pe hop, phir opposite leg pe step, phir jump
  • Board se measurement same as long jump

FIELD EVENTS - Throwing

1. Shot Put

Weight: Men 7.26kg | Women 4kg

Circle: 2.135m diameter

Technique: Shot ko neck ke paas hold karo, throw nahi - PUT karna hai

Foul: Circle se bahar nikle throw ke baad, ya top of stop board pe step kiya

Sector: 34.92° angle mein girna chahiye

2. Discus Throw

  • Weight: Men 2kg | Women 1kg
  • Circle: 2.5m diameter
  • Cage: Safety net around circle
  • Sector: 34.92° angle

3. Javelin Throw

  • Weight: Men 800g | Women 600g
  • Runway: 30-36.5m long, 4m wide
  • Tip First: Javelin ka tip pehle ground touch karna chahiye
  • No Turn: Back throw ke time opponent ki taraf nahi dekhna
  • Sector: 29° angle
