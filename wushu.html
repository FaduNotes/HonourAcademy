Wushu Kya Hai?

Wushu China ka 5000+ saal purana martial art hai. Modern Wushu do categories mein divide hai: Taolu (Forms) aur Sanda (Full Contact Fighting).

Speciality: Acrobatics, flexibility, speed aur beauty ka combination. Kung Fu movies mein jo dekhte ho wo Wushu hai.

TAOLU - Forms Competition

1. Barehand Forms

  • Changquan: Long Fist - Northern style, acrobatic, extended movements, high kicks, jumping
  • Nanquan: Southern Fist - Low stances, powerful, short range, strong arms
  • Taijiquan: Tai Chi - Slow, flowing, internal energy, meditation

2. Weapons Forms

  • Jianshu: Straight Sword - elegant, precise
  • Daoshu: Broadsword - powerful, aggressive
  • Qiangshu: Spear - long range, thrusting
  • Gunshu: Staff - 6 feet long, spinning techniques
  • Nandao: Southern Broadsword
  • Nangun: Southern Staff

3. Taolu Judging Criteria

Quality of Movements (5.0): Technique, Stance, Balance, Coordination

Overall Performance (3.0): Rhythm, Power, Spirit, Choreography

Difficulty (2.0): Jump height, Spinning, Combinations

Deductions: Balance loss -0.1, Weapon drop -0.5, Time violation -0.1 per 5 sec

Time Limit: 1 min 20 sec (minimum) to 2 min (maximum)

4. Required Movements in Taolu

Har form mein ye Nandu (Difficulty) movements hone chahiye:

  • Jumps: Butterfly kick, Tornado kick, 540/720 kicks
  • Balances: One leg stand 3 sec minimum
  • Stances: Ma Bu (Horse), Gong Bu (Bow), Pu Bu (Crouch)
  • Hand Techniques: Palm strikes, Fist, Claw

SANDA - Full Contact Fighting

1. Match Format

Rounds: 3 rounds x 2 minutes (Amateur) | 5 rounds x 3 minutes (Pro)

Ring: 8m x 8m platform (Leitai) - 60cm height

Equipment: Gloves, Head guard, Chest guard, Shin guards, Mouthguard, Groin guard

2. Scoring System

  • Punch to head/body: 1 point
  • Kick to body: 1 point
  • Kick to head: 2 points
  • Throw/Takedown: 2 points (opponent falls, you remain standing)
  • Push opponent off platform: 2 points
  • Opponent falls (you also fall): 1 point

3. Legal Techniques

  • Punches: Jab, Cross, Hook, Uppercut
  • Kicks: All types - front, side, roundhouse, spinning
  • Throws: Hip throw, Shoulder throw, Leg sweep
  • Takedowns: Single leg, Double leg
  • Clinch: 3 seconds max, then knee strikes allowed

4. Illegal Techniques - Fouls

✗ Elbow strikes

✗ Knee to head (body allowed in pro)

✗ Attack to back of head, throat, groin, spine

✗ Joint locks, Chokes

✗ Headbutt, Biting

✗ Attacking downed opponent

✗ Holding ropes/ring

Penalty: Warning → -1 point → -2 points → Disqualification

5. Winning Methods

  • Win by Points: 3 rounds ke baad zyada points
  • Win by KO/TKO: Knockout ya Referee stop
  • Win by 2 Knockdowns: Ek round mein 2 baar giraya
  • Win by Throw Out: 2 baar platform se bahar push kiya
  • Absolute Victory: 12 point lead = match khatam
