Kickboxing Ke Styles

1. American Kickboxing

Above waist kicks only. No knees, no elbows, no clinch. Clean sport.

2. Muay Thai (Thai Boxing)

"Art of 8 Limbs" - Punches, Kicks, Knees, Elbows sab allowed. Clinch fighting bhi. Most brutal style.

3. K-1 Rules

Knees allowed, limited clinch (1 knee only). No elbows. Most popular pro rules.

4. Dutch Kickboxing

Heavy combinations, pressure fighting. Low kicks allowed. Glory Kickboxing ka style.

K-1 Rules - Hum Ye Follow Karte Hai

1. Match Format

Rounds: 3 rounds x 3 minutes (Amateur) | 3-5 rounds x 3 minutes (Pro)

Rest: 1 minute between rounds

Ring: Boxing ring ya 6m x 6m mat

Equipment: Gloves (10oz), Mouthguard, Groin guard, Shin guards (amateur)

2. Scoring System

  • Clean Punch: 1 point
  • Clean Kick to body/legs: 1 point
  • Clean Kick to head: 2 points
  • Jumping/Spinning Kick to head: 3 points
  • Knee to body: 1 point
  • Knockdown: 8-count, opponent ko 1 point deduction

3. Legal Techniques

  • Punches: Jab, Cross, Hook, Uppercut, Spinning Backfist
  • Kicks: Low kick (thigh), Middle kick (body), High kick (head)
  • Types: Front kick, Roundhouse, Side kick, Hook kick, Axe kick
  • Spinning: Spinning back kick, Spinning hook kick
  • Jumping: Flying knee, Jumping kicks
  • Knees: To body only, 1 knee in clinch then break

4. Illegal Techniques - Fouls

✗ Elbow strikes (K-1 mein illegal)

✗ Knee to head

✗ Clinch more than 5 seconds

✗ Holding and hitting

✗ Throws, Takedowns, Sweeps

✗ Headbutt, Biting

✗ Attack to groin, back of head, spine

✗ Attacking downed opponent

Penalty: Warning → Yellow Card (-1 point) → Red Card (DQ)

5. Winning Methods

  • Win by Decision: 3 rounds ke baad points
  • Win by KO: Knockout - 10 count
  • Win by TKO: 3 knockdowns in 1 round OR referee stop
  • Win by Retirement: Corner throws towel

Training Structure

1. Warm-up (15 min)

Skipping, Shadow boxing, Dynamic stretching

2. Technique (20 min)

New combinations, Pad work with coach, Heavy bag

3. Conditioning (15 min)

Burpees, Mountain climbers, Planks, Sprints

4. Sparring (20 min)

Light contact for beginners, Full contact for fighters. Safety gear mandatory.

5. Cool Down (10 min)

Static stretching, Breathing exercises

Grading System

White Belt: Beginner (0-3 months)

Yellow Belt: Basic punches & kicks (3-6 months)

Orange Belt: Combinations (6-9 months)

Green Belt: Advanced techniques (9-12 months)

Blue Belt: Sparring ready (1-1.5 years)

Brown Belt: Fighter level (1.5-2 years)

Black Belt: Instructor level (2+ years)

Black Belt Exam: 2 min shadow, 3 rounds sparring, 50 pushups, 100 sit-ups, board breaking
