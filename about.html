Our Story

Honour Academy was founded by Philiaalex with a single mission - to make Daman & Diu stronger, fitter, and more confident. What started as a small dojo has now become the region's most trusted name in Martial Arts and Fitness training.

Philiaalex's Vision

"Martial arts is not just about fighting, it's about building character. At Honour Academy, we don't just train your body, we train your mind. Every student who walks through our doors learns discipline, respect, and the courage to face any challenge in life."
- Philiaalex, Founder

Why Train With Us

1. Leadership of Philiaalex: Direct guidance from the founder who is a certified black belt and national-level athlete.

2. Proven System: Training methodology developed by Philiaalex over 10+ years, creating champions at district and state level.
