Taekwondo Kya Hai?

Taekwondo Korea ka national martial art aur Olympic sport hai. "Tae" = Kick, "Kwon" = Punch, "Do" = Way. Matlab "The way of foot and fist".

Speciality: Duniya ka sabse zyada kicks wala martial art. 80% techniques kicks hai - high, fast, spinning kicks.

Taekwondo Ke 2 Main Parts

1. Kyorugi - Sparring/Fighting

Full contact fighting with electronic scoring system. Olympic event hai.

2. Poomsae - Forms

Pre-arranged movements imaginary opponent ke against. Style, power, balance judge hota hai.

Kyorugi Rules - Olympic Sparring

1. Match Format

Rounds: 3 rounds x 2 minutes | 1 minute rest

Ring: 8m x 8m octagonal mat

Equipment: Hogu (chest guard), Head guard, Shin guards, Forearm guards, Gloves, Mouthguard, Groin guard

2. Scoring System - Electronic System

  • Punch to Hogu: 1 point
  • Kick to Hogu: 2 points
  • Spinning Kick to Hogu: 4 points
  • Kick to Head: 3 points
  • Spinning Kick to Head: 5 points
  • Gam-jeom (Penalty): Opponent ko +1 point

3. Legal Techniques

  • Punches: Straight punch to Hogu only - face pe punch illegal
  • Kicks: Front kick, Roundhouse, Side kick, Back kick, Hook kick, Axe kick
  • Spinning Kicks: Tornado, 540, 720 kicks - high points

4. Prohibited Acts - Gam-jeom

✗ Grabbing/Holding opponent

✗ Pushing opponent out of ring

✗ Kicking below waist

✗ Attacking fallen opponent

✗ Punch to face/head

✗ Attacking after Kal-yeo (Stop)

✗ Falling down intentionally

10 Gam-jeom = Automatic Disqualification

5. Winning Methods

  • Win by Points: 3 rounds ke baad zyada points
  • Win by Point Gap: 20 point difference = match khatam
  • Win by RSC: Referee Stop Contest - injury ya 8-count
  • Win by KO: Knockout
  • Golden Point: Tie pe 4th round - pehla point jeetega

Poomsae - Forms Competition

1. Taegeuk Poomsae (Color Belts)

8 Taegeuk forms - Taegeuk 1 se Taegeuk 8 tak. Har belt pe naya form.

2. Black Belt Poomsae

  • Koryo: 1st Dan
  • Keumgang: 2nd Dan
  • Taebaek: 3rd Dan
  • Pyongwon: 4th Dan
  • Sipjin: 5th Dan

3. Poomsae Judging

Accuracy (4.0): Correct techniques, stances

Presentation (6.0): Power, Speed, Rhythm, Expression

Total: 10.0 points

Belt System - Geup & Dan

White: 10th Geup - Beginner

Yellow: 8th Geup (3 months)

Green: 6th Geup (6 months)

Blue: 4th Geup (9 months)

Red: 2nd Geup (1 year)

Black Belt: 1st Dan to 9th Dan

Black Belt Exam: 1st Dan ke liye minimum 3 years training + board breaking + sparring + poomsae

Board Breaking - Kyukpa

Black belt exam mein wooden boards todne padte hai:

  • 1st Dan: 1 board - Hand/Foot
  • 2nd Dan: 2 boards - Different techniques
  • 3rd Dan: 3 boards - Spinning/Jumping kicks
