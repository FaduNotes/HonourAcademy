Pencak Silat Kya Hai?

Pencak Silat Indonesia ka 2000+ saal purana martial art hai. "Pencak" matlab artistic performance aur "Silat" matlab fighting art. Ye UNESCO World Heritage bhi hai.

Speciality: Sabse versatile martial art - strikes, kicks, throws, locks, ground fighting, weapons - sab kuch ek system mein.

Silat Ke 4 Main Aspects

1. Seni - Artistic Performance

Jurus (forms) ka performance music ke saath. Grace, flow, power dikhana hota hai. Tunggal, Ganda, Regu categories hote hai.

2. Beladiri - Self Defense

Real-life attack se bachne ki techniques. Multiple attackers, weapon defense sab sikhate hai.

3. Olahraga - Sport

Competition fighting. Point system se match hota hai.

4. Mental Spiritual

Breathing, meditation, inner power development. Tenaga Dalam (inner energy) training.

Tanding Rules - Sport Silat Competition

1. Match Format

Rounds: 3 rounds x 2 minutes | 1 minute rest

Arena: 10m x 10m mat

Win By: Points, TKO, Disqualification, Withdrawal

2. Scoring System

  • Punch/Kick to body: 1 point
  • Kick to head: 2 points
  • Successful throw: 3 points
  • Lock/Hold 5 sec: 4 points
  • Knockdown: 5 points

3. Allowed Techniques

  • Punches: Jab, Straight, Hook, Uppercut
  • Kicks: Front, Side, Roundhouse, Spinning kicks
  • Throws: Hip throw, Shoulder throw, Sweep
  • Locks: Arm lock, Leg lock, Neck lock
  • Takedowns: Single leg, Double leg

4. Prohibited - Fouls

✗ Eyes, Throat, Groin, Spine pe attack

✗ Biting, Scratching, Hair pulling

✗ Elbow to head

✗ Knee to head when opponent is down

✗ Attacking after "Berhenti" (Stop) command

Penalty: Warning → -1 point → -2 points → Disqualification

12 Basic Jurus - Forms

Har Silat student ko 12 basic Jurus seekhne padte hai. Har Jurus mein 8-12 movements hote hai:

  • Jurus 1-3: Basic stance, punches, blocks
  • Jurus 4-6: Kicks, combinations
  • Jurus 7-9: Throws, takedowns
  • Jurus 10-12: Advanced locks, counters

Weapons Training - Senjata

Advanced level pe traditional weapons:

  • Keris: Wavy dagger - Indonesian national weapon
  • Golok: Machete - cutting techniques
  • Toya: Long staff - 6 feet
  • Karambit: Curved knife - close combat
  • Trisula: Three-pronged spear

Note: Weapons training sirf demonstration ke liye, blunt weapons use hote hai safety ke liye.

Belt System - Tingkatan

White: Pemula - Beginner (0-6 months)

Yellow: Dasar I (6-12 months)

Green: Dasar II (1-2 years)

Blue: Madya I (2-3 years)

Brown: Madya II (3-4 years)

Black: Pendekar - Expert (5+ years) | 1st to 9th Dan
