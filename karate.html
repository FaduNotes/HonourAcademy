Karate Kya Hai?

Karate "Empty Hand" ka matlab hai - bina hathiyar ke ladne ki kala. Shotokan Karate style hum sikhate hai jo Gichin Funakoshi ne banaya tha - duniya ka sabse popular karate form.

Philosophy: "Karate ni sente nashi" - Karate mein pehla attack nahi hota. Self-defense aur character building pe focus.

Karate Ke 3 Main Parts

1. KIHON - Basics

Basic techniques ka practice. Stances, punches, kicks, blocks. Foundation strong hona chahiye.

  • Dachi (Stances): Zenkutsu-dachi, Kokutsu-dachi, Kiba-dachi
  • Tsuki (Punches): Oi-zuki, Gyaku-zuki, Kizami-zuki
  • Geri (Kicks): Mae-geri, Yoko-geri, Mawashi-geri, Ushiro-geri
  • Uke (Blocks): Age-uke, Soto-uke, Uchi-uke, Gedan-barai

2. KATA - Forms

Pre-arranged movements imaginary opponents ke against. 26 Shotokan Kata hote hai. Har belt pe naye kata.

Heian Shodan: White-Yellow Belt - 21 movements

Heian Nidan: Yellow-Orange Belt - 26 movements

Heian Sandan: Orange-Green Belt - 20 movements

Heian Yondan: Green-Blue Belt - 27 movements

Heian Godan: Blue-Brown Belt - 23 movements

Tekki Shodan: Brown Belt - 29 movements

Black Belt Kata: Bassai Dai, Kanku Dai, Jion, Empi, etc.

3. KUMITE - Sparring/Fighting

Real opponent ke saath fight practice. 5 types:

  • Gohon Kumite: 5-step sparring - Beginners
  • Sanbon Kumite: 3-step sparring
  • Kihon Ippon Kumite: 1-step sparring
  • Jiyu Ippon Kumite: Semi-free sparring
  • Jiyu Kumite: Free sparring - Tournament style

KUMITE RULES - WKF Competition

1. Match Format

Duration: 3 minutes (Men) | 2 minutes (Women) | No break

Area: 8m x 8m matted area

Win By: 8 points lead OR most points after time OR Hansoku (DQ) OR Kiken (forfeit)

2. Scoring System

  • IPPON (3 points): Jodan kicks (head), Any technique on thrown/fallen opponent
  • WAZA-ARI (2 points): Chudan kicks (body)
  • YUKO (1 point): Chudan ya Jodan punch, Any strike to body

3. Scoring Criteria - Technique Must Have:

  • Good Form: Proper technique
  • Sporting Attitude: Fair play
  • Vigorous Application: Power + Speed
  • Zanshin: Awareness after attack
  • Good Timing: Perfect moment
  • Correct Distance: Not too close, not too far

4. Prohibited Techniques - Fouls

✗ Excessive Contact: Injury causing hit

✗ Attacks to: Throat, Arms, Legs, Groin, Joints

✗ Open Hand Techniques: Palm, Finger strikes to face

✗ Dangerous Throws: Landing on head/neck

✗ Mubobi: Lack of self-defense - defending nahi karna

✗ Jogai: Area se bahar nikalna

✗ Waste of Time: Fighting avoid karna

Penalty: Chukoku (Warning) → Keikoku → Hansoku-Chui → Hansoku (DQ)

DOJO KUN - Dojo Rules & Etiquette

  • Rei: Dojo mein enter/exit karte time bow karo
  • "Oss": Sensei/Senior ki baat ka jawab "Oss" bol ke do
  • Seiza: Line mein baithne ka proper tareeka
  • Gi: Karate dress hamesha saaf rakho, properly wear karo
  • Nails: Short rakho - safety ke liye
  • Jewellery: Remove karo training se pehle
  • Late Aana: Seiza mein baitho, Sensei ki permission leke join karo
  • Respect: Seniors ko respect, Juniors ko help karo

Black Belt Exam Requirements - Shodan (1st Dan)

✓ Kihon: All basic techniques combination

✓ Kata: Heian 1-5 + Tekki Shodan + 1 Black belt kata (Bassai Dai)

✓ Kumite: Jiyu Ippon + Jiyu Kumite 3 rounds

✓ Board Breaking: 1 inch board with punch/kick

✓ Written Test: Karate history, terminology, philosophy

✓ Minimum Age: 16 years

✓ Training Time: Minimum 4-5 years from White belt

Board Breaking - Tameshiwari

Power testing ke liye wooden boards todne padte hai:

  • Green Belt: 1 board - Hand technique
  • Blue Belt: 1 board - Foot technique
  • Brown Belt: 2 boards - Any technique
  • Black Belt: 2-3 boards - Spinning/Jumping technique
