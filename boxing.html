Boxing Kya Hai?

Boxing combat sport hai jisme sirf punches use hote hai. Olympic sport bhi hai aur professional career ke liye best. "Sweet Science" kehte hai isko - technique + strategy + stamina ka game.

Speciality: Best for hand-eye coordination, reflexes, fitness. Self-defense ke liye sabse practical.

Boxing Ke 4 Basic Punches

1. Jab

Lead hand se straight punch. Range measure karne aur opponent ko disturb karne ke liye. Sabse zyada use hota hai.

2. Cross (Straight Right)

Rear hand se power punch. Sabse strong punch boxing ka. Jab ke baad use karte hai - "1-2 combination".

3. Hook

Side se curved punch. Head ya body pe. Liver shot (left hook to body) se KO ho jata hai.

4. Uppercut

Neeche se upar ki taraf punch. Chin pe lagta hai. Close range ka weapon.

Boxing Rules - Amateur/Olympic

1. Match Format

Rounds: 3 rounds x 3 minutes (Men) | 3 rounds x 2 minutes (Women)

Rest: 1 minute between rounds

Ring: 6.1m x 6.1m square

Equipment: Gloves (10oz), Headgear, Mouthguard, Groin guard, Boxing shoes

2. Scoring System - 10-Point Must

  • Clean Punch: Glove ka white part se hit, power ke saath
  • Target Area: Head (front/side), Body (above belt)
  • Judging: 5 judges, 3 ka score count hota hai
  • Winner: 10 points, Loser: 9 ya kam
  • Knockdown: Automatically 10-8 round

3. Legal Techniques

  • Closed fist punches only
  • Hit with knuckle part of glove
  • Both hands use kar sakte hai
  • Clinch allowed but referee breaks immediately

4. Illegal Moves - Fouls

✗ Below belt hit (Low blow)

✗ Back of head hit (Rabbit punch)

✗ Kidney punch

✗ Holding and hitting

✗ Headbutt, Elbow, Kicking

✗ Hitting after Bell/Break command

✗ Spitting mouthguard

Penalty: Warning → Point deduction → Disqualification

5. Winning Methods

  • Win by Points (WP): 3 rounds ke baad decision
  • Win by RSC: Referee Stop Contest - injury ya one-sided
  • Win by KO: Knockout - 10 count tak nahi utha
  • Win by RSC-I: Injury se stop
  • Win by ABD: Abandon - opponent gave up
  • Win by DQ: Disqualification

Defensive Techniques

  • Slip: Head move karke punch miss karana
  • Duck: Neeche jhukna
  • Block: Gloves se punch rokna
  • Parry: Punch ko deflect karna
  • Footwork: Ring mein move karna - sabse important
  • Clinch: Opponent ko pakad ke rest lena
